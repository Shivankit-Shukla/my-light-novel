*When the hero who gave his all to save the world was subjected to despair, wrath of the people and fear of those whom he saved from the cosmic curses, he didn't just succumb as this was not what he seeked for. So, the journey of total erasure was what had been left for the cruel people and the hero himself.*

Welcome to the official repository for VOIDBORN, an upcoming dark, psychological Seinen-style light novel.

This repository serves as an open-source production log detailing a comprehensive six month journey into dark fantasy world-building, narrative architecture, and advanced plot. Every line of script, conceptual breakdown, and structural pipeline asset will be documented openly right here.

Thematic Core:

-The Cruelty of Irony: Exploring the psychological fracture that occurs when the ultimate protectors of a realm become the perpetrators of its absolute deepest tragedy.

-The Birth of an Anti-Hero: A raw, unfiltered exploration of grief mutating into static, calculated malice. This is not a journey of healing—it is a study of a man becoming a force of nature.

-The Weight of the Void: Visually and narratively translating abstract human suffering into an all-consuming, physical destructive element.
