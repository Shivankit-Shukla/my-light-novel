
# Void Metaphysical Rules

This document establishes the absolute physical and mental laws governing Void energy within the world. All abilities, weapons, and narrative stakes must align with these core boundaries.

---

## 1. The Nature of the Void
The Void is not merely empty space; it is a volatile, high-pressure dimension that exists parallel to our reality, occupying "the space between heartbeats." 

To an outside observer, it looks like a pitch-black, shimmering vacuum. For a user, tapping into the Void means briefly pulling that volatile environment into the physical world, manipulating gravity, space, and kinetic force.

---

## 2. The Three Laws of Void Energy

### I. The Law of Displacement
Void energy cannot coexist with ordinary matter. When a user summons a Void rift or ability, it instantly displaces the air and physical objects in that exact space, creating a localized vacuum. 

### II. The Law of Focus
The Void is inherently chaotic and constantly seeks to collapse back into its own dimension. A manifestation only remains stable as long as the user maintains absolute, unbroken mental focus. If concentration wavers for a microsecond, the energy instantly implodes.

### III. The Law of Thermal Drain
The Void is completely devoid of thermodynamic energy. Activating a Void ability instantly absorbs the ambient heat from the surrounding environment, causing the temperature to plummet rapidly wherever an ability is used.

---

## 3. The Cost of Use (The Backlash)

Manipulating a reality-warping dimension takes a heavy toll on a human body. Power is strictly bound to physical cost.

* **Short-Term Strain:** Using the Void causes a severe spike in blood pressure and rapid physical exhaustion. Prolonged deployment during a fight leads to temporary sensory burnout—specifically causing temporary color-blindness and a ringing in the ears (tinnitus) that lasts for several minutes after the ability ends.
* **The Long-Term Toll:** The human nervous system was not built to channel vacuum energy. Over time, frequent users develop permanent physical scarring along their veins, appearing as faint, geometric silver lines beneath the skin where the energy traveled.

---

## 4. Manifestation Phases

Every active use of a Void ability follows a strict three-step sequence:

1. **The Breach (Perception):** The user slows their heart rate down to perceive the thin boundary line between realities.
2. **The Distortion (Manipulation):** The user bridges the gap, allowing Void energy to bleed into the physical world to warp space, create shields, or deliver kinetic strikes.
3. **The Collapse (Stabilization):** The energy snaps shut back into its own dimension, resulting in a sudden rush of rushing air (a kinetic clap) as nature rushes to fill the vacuum left behind.
